⚠️ Nota Penting Multiplier

Contoh multiplier hanya untuk rujukan.
Jangan teka multiplier sebenar berdasarkan jenis premis sahaja. Sila sahkan nilai sebenar daripada nameplate CT/VT, setting meter, label panel, sistem bil, SLD, test block label atau rekod commissioning.

Multiplier examples are for reference only.
Always verify the actual multiplier from CT/VT nameplate, meter setting, panel label, billing system, SLD, test block label, or commissioning record.

🧮 Formula Multiplier

Mode Formula
Direct Meter M = 1
CT Meter M = CT Primary ÷ CT Secondary
CT + VT Meter M = (CT Primary ÷ CT Secondary) × (VT Primary ÷ VT Secondary)

❓ Jika CT/VT Tidak Diketahui

Jika CT primary atau VT primary tidak diketahui, jangan teka multiplier. Gunakan nilai Known Multiplier jika ada daripada setting meter, label panel, sistem bil, SLD, test block label, atau rekod commissioning.

Maklumat Yang Ada Cadangan Mode
Tahu CT ratio sahaja CT Meter
Tahu CT ratio dan VT ratio CT + VT Meter
Tahu multiplier sahaja Known Multiplier
Tidak tahu CT/VT dan tidak tahu multiplier Tidak boleh kira tenaga sebenar

Tanpa CT/VT ratio atau multiplier sebenar, app hanya boleh kira nilai secondary/reference sahaja, bukan tenaga sebenar di primary side.

🔁 Reverse Check Jika Multiplier Diketahui

Tujuan Formula
Cari CT Primary jika tiada VT CT Primary = M × CT Secondary
Cari CT Primary jika ada VT CT Primary = M ÷ VT Ratio × CT Secondary
Cari VT Primary jika CT Ratio diketahui VT Primary = (M ÷ CT Ratio) × VT Secondary

Contoh: M = 80,000, CT Secondary = 1A, VT = 11kV/110V. VT Ratio = 100. CT Primary = 80,000 ÷ 100 × 1 = 800A.
